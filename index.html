Exploring Sora: AI-Generated Video

What is Sora?

Sora is an emerging technology developed by OpenAI that can create realistic and imaginative scenes from text instructions. It represents a massive leap in how we create digital media.

Social & Ethical Impacts

While Sora democratizes creativity, it also raises concerns about misinformation and the future of the film industry.
